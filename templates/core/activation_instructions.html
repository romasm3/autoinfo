Get started with vehicle history reports in 4 easy steps

1

Create Your Account

Visit AutoInfo and click on the Register button. Fill in your email address and create a secure password. You'll be automatically logged in after registration.

  • Enter your email address
  • Create a strong password (min. 8 characters)
  • Confirm your password
  • Accept the Terms of Service
  • Click "Register" button
Registration Form Screenshot
üí° Pro Tip: Use a valid email address as you'll receive important notifications about your reports.
2

Add Credits to Your Account

After registration, you need to add credits to your account to generate reports. Click on the "Buy Credits" button in your dashboard sidebar.

  • Click "Buy credits" in the sidebar
  • Choose your report type (Carfax, Autocheck, or NMVTIS)
  • Select package size (1, 10, or 100 reports)
  • Complete payment via Stripe (credit card)
  • Credits will be instantly added to your account
Buy Credits Page Screenshot
üí° Pro Tip: Larger packages (10 or 100 reports) offer better prices per report. Save up to 33%!
3

Generate Your Vehicle Report

Now you're ready to generate reports! In your dashboard, locate the "Generate report" section.

  • Enter the 17-character VIN number
  • Select report type (Carfax, Autocheck, or NMVTIS)
  • Click the "Generate" button
  • Wait a few seconds for the report to be generated
  • Your credits will be automatically deducted
VIN Search Form Screenshot
üí° Pro Tip: VIN numbers cannot contain the letters I, O, or Q to avoid confusion with numbers 1 and 0.
4

View & Download Your Report

Your report will appear in the "Last 10 reports" table at the bottom of the dashboard. Reports are generated instantly and available immediately.

  • Scroll down to the "Last 10 reports" table
  • Find your VIN in the list
  • Click the blue "Display ‚Üì" button
  • View the complete report with all vehicle history
  • You can print or save the report as PDF
