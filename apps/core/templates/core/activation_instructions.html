Activation instruction

1. Registration

Register by entering your name, email address, and password - you will be automatically redirected to your panel.

2. Add credits

Click "Buy credits" in the sidebar, choose your package, and complete payment via Stripe. Credits will be instantly added to your account.

3. Report generation

Enter the VIN number of the vehicle, select the report type and press the "Generate" button.

4. Displaying the report

The report will be available at the bottom of the page under the blue "Display report" button within a few seconds.
